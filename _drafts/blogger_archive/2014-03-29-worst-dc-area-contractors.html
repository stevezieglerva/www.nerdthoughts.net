--- layout: post title: Worst DC Area Contractors - Visualizing BBB Complaints date: '2014-03-29T10:38:00.002-04:00' author: Stephen Ziegler tags: - home modified_time: '2014-03-29T10:44:39.090-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1727955271917225446.post-3148726574544509602 blogger_orig_url: http://www.nerdthoughts.net/2014/03/worst-dc-area-contractors.html --- While you can use sites like Angie's List and Yelp to learn about the quality and reputation of contractors, I find information from the Better Business Bureau to be a really good indicator. If you're going to take the time to fill out the forms for the BBB, you've probably really been wronged.

While the BBB gives you number of complaints about each individual contractor, it does not show data across contractors to compare. So, if you see a contractor has 5 complaints in the last 3 years, you're not sure if 5 is average or terrible compared to their peers.

So, I page-scraped the data for the DC area region and visualized it with Tableau. Here is a tree map of the complaints. I also created a dashboard with linked pie chart as well. For plumbers, you can see the Michael and Son Services, My Plumber, and Parrish Services by far have the most complaints.
