--- layout: post title: Is "hair" trending on DC Sports Bog again? date: '2015-09-05T09:00:00.000-04:00' author: Stephen Ziegler tags: modified_time: '2015-09-05T09:25:46.394-04:00' thumbnail: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-uwoBW5xDeWE/VePKheEYWHI/AAAAAAAAhJQ/uXMhdZFwY7g/s72-c/DC%2BSports%2BBog.png blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1727955271917225446.post-6904597477918481695 blogger_orig_url: http://www.nerdthoughts.net/2015/09/is-hair-trending-on-dc-sports-bog-again.html --- The DC Sports Bog by Dan Steinberg has a really unique take on DC sports. I guess you'd have to have a unique take after living with Dan Snyder's ownership for the last 16 years. He takes a snarky, cynical view of the teams, their owners, and media relations.

I've been exploring classifiers for unstructured text and decided to try one out on the blog. I'm looking for a simple classifier that won't required a big effort in developing training data for learning. I downloaded all of the posts from 2014 and tried to classify posts into a few categories that any reader of the blog will recognize:
  • Injury - Big topic these days with RG3's non-concussion (verbal)/concussion (written)
  • Broadcaster - Dan talks a lot about local sports broadcasters
  • Hair - Facial, head, and chest
  • Losing - I was curious how much losing was talked about, especially given some crushing playoff losses recently
  • Owners - Seems like posts about Dan Snyder write themselves
  • Politics - You can't be in DC without writing about politicians talking about the local teams
Instead of just searching and counting individual words in the text, I used term frequencies against a corpus of typical blog posts to extract keywords. This allowed me to determine what the post was about. I then searched these keywords for specified words related to a category:
  • hair|Is about: haircut, hair, beard, mustache, goatee
  • injury|Is about: injuries, injury, hurt
  • losing|Is about: losing, loss
  • broadcasters|Is about: dianna russini, dan hellie, britt mchenry, dave owens, kristen berset, tim brant, lindsay czarniak
  • owners|Is about: daniel snyder, dan snyder, ted leonsis
  • politics|Is about: congressman, senator , governor, president obama, vice president biden, george bush, barrack obama, joe biden, hillary clinton, bill clinton, muriel bowser, marion barry, harry reid, nancy pelosi, john boehner
The rules for the categories aren't perfect, but they do give you a quick way to get decent classification of the text. 

I then created some small multiples to show the references for the categories over time. As I expected, there are a lot of posts about the owners. Surprisingly though, there seem to be more posts about Leonsis, than Snyder.  Maybe it's because Ted owns two local teams.  I was not surprised to see the constant flow of posts about hair. Dan loves a good beard or 'stashe.
