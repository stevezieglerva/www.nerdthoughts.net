He had a great quote:


I loved this quote when I heard it and find its meaning cropping up at work all of the time. I also find myself inserting words into the quote:
  • Shared status reports aren't shared understanding.
  • Share project plans aren't shared understanding.
  • Shared emails aren't shared understanding.
  • Shared phone calls aren't shared understanding.
So, remember this quote the next time you need a group of people to truly understand something.
