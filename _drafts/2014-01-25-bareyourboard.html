A recent creative ideas is their Bare Your Board contest which encourages current clients to show pictures of their boards to win a Amazon gift card or GoPro video camera.  To enter, you have to answer some questions about how you use the product and submit a picture of the board.  It's a great idea for several reasons:

  • Shows the product being used in real life situations -  Their customers are using the product to run projects, businesses, and their lives.  
  • Uses testimonials from real customers - Being a 50% sales person myself, I don't like other sales people.  I typically see through the BS and want to get straight to the facts. This lets potential customers see real problem-solving value without the typical marketing spin.
  • Promotes Kanban through examples - Leankit is very active in the Agile community and believes in the power of Agile and Lean thinking. When I was learning Kanban, seeing example boards was very educational. I still love seeing real boards.
PS. I realize this contest may be dated now.  It took me a while to find time to write about it.
